Who we are

Kapriol has equipped professionals across Europe since 1974.

Founded in Italy in 1974, Kapriol builds workwear, footwear and protective equipment for people who work with their hands. Five decades of field-tested gear, engineered to outlast the job.

In Bulgaria, Kapriol is distributed exclusively by ergotrade.bg. Every item in this catalog is sourced direct, held in local stock and backed by a team that knows the trades it supplies.

From the first layer to the last line of defence, the standard does not move.
